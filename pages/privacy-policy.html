Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to PYQ. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, and process your files and data when you browse our search engine or contribute resources.

Information We Process

As a decentralized, student-driven repository, we do not require you to create an account or provide personal identification to search or download question papers.

  • Uploaded Files: When you upload a previous year question paper, we collect the PDF file, the custom title you assign, and the selected exam type. These files are stored on our servers and made publicly downloadable.
  • Local Storage: We may use browser local storage to save your user interface preferences (such as light/dark mode settings).
  • Server Logs: Our local backend server logs incoming network requests, including IP address, user-agent details, and timestamp, purely for security monitoring and resource abuse prevention.

Security Measures

We restrict uploaded files only to the PDF format (`.pdf`) to protect our users from malicious executables, script injections, or file corruption. All files uploaded to PYQ are isolated on the filesystem.

Content Removal (Takedown Requests)

If you are a copyright owner, academic institution, or student who believes that a document uploaded to PYQ infringes your intellectual property or contains private personal details, please contact us immediately through our Contact Us page. We will process and take down the infringing content within 24–48 hours.

Changes to Policy

We reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon updating this document page.
