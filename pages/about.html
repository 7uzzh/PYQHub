About PYQ

Our Mission

PYQ is India's largest and most modern search engine for Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs). Our goal is to consolidate resource access and remove hurdles for millions of aspirants preparing for board exams, university degrees, and competitive assessments (like BPSC, SSC, and Railway).

We believe that education resources should be freely and instantly accessible to everyone without paywalls, heavy registration forms, or spam advertisements.

Why Choose PYQ?

  • Instant Dynamic Search: Query and filter thousands of papers in milliseconds using keyword search.
  • Student-Driven Database: Students can drag-and-drop papers to upload them, going live instantly for the entire community.
  • No Ad Spam: Enjoy a clean, distraction-free environment to retrieve documents.
  • Clean PDFs: Standardized documents curated and verified for educational use.

Join the Community

This platform is built for students, by students. If you have question papers from your past semesters, board exams, or competitive exams, upload them today! Your contribution will help someone else prepare and succeed.
